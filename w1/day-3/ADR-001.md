# ADR-001: Selection of Monitoring Infrastructure (Prometheus vs. Datadog)

**Date:** 2026-06-03
**Status:** Accepted

## Context
Our platform requires a robust monitoring and observability solution to track AIOps pipeline performance, system health, and service-level objectives (SLOs). We need to decide between a self-hosted Prometheus instance or a managed SaaS solution like Datadog. Factors include operational overhead, total cost of ownership (TCO), and performance latency.

## Decision
We have decided to adopt **Prometheus** (deployed via Helm on our Kubernetes cluster) combined with Grafana for visualization.

## Consequences
### Positive
- **Cost Efficiency:** No per-host or per-metric ingestion fees. Operating costs are limited to cloud infrastructure resources (EKS/EC2).
- **Data Sovereignty:** Metrics data remains within our VPC, ensuring compliance with strict data security requirements.
- **Latency:** Local network scraping ensures low latency for critical alerting, typically under 100ms for metric collection loops.

### Negative
- **Operational Overhead:** Requires manual maintenance of retention policies, storage scaling (e.g., using Thanos or Cortex for long-term storage), and uptime monitoring of the monitoring stack itself.

## Alternatives
### Datadog
- **Pros:** Zero maintenance, sophisticated AIOps features, integrated APM, and distributed tracing.
- **Cons:** High variable cost. At our scale (approx. 500 nodes), Datadog pricing models suggest a TCO of ~$15,000/month, compared to ~$1,200/month for running managed EKS nodes for a self-hosted Prometheus stack.
- **Latency:** Ingestion delay can reach 3-5 seconds depending on regional load, which is higher than local Prometheus scraping.

## Quantified Trade-offs
- **Monthly Cost:** Prometheus (~$1,200/mo) vs. Datadog (~$15,000/mo) - Savings of >90%.
- **Retention:** Prometheus local storage is optimized for 15 days; beyond this, we incur additional storage costs for S3-backed long-term storage (approx. $50/TB), whereas Datadog scales linearly with retention duration.
- **Collection Latency:** Prometheus scrape latency is consistently <100ms; Datadog agent reporting latency averages 2-5s.
